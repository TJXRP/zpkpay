Dual Rail Settlement ?Two things happen with one signature. Rail 1 is the XRPL payment - money moves on the ledger. Rail 2 is the ISO message - it is shattered into fragments and stored in your Immutable Storage Cluster. The payment and the message are cryptographically bound together. They cannot be separated or disputed. How the receiver authenticates to retrieve the message is fully configurable - single wallet, multisig, hardware key, or any method your compliance team requires.

Calculated ZPK™ DNA Anchor ?A DNA Anchor is a unique 32-byte fingerprint of your ISO message. It proves the message existed at a specific moment in time without revealing any of its contents. In this demo it is stored in the XRPL memo field. In your enterprise deployment it can go on your private ledger, internal database, or any write-once system your compliance team approves.
The DNA Anchor can be placed on any public ledger or stored entirely in-house, it's completely agnostic.
